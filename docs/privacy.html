Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

FoodPool ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address (used for authentication and account recovery)
  • Your full name (displayed only to you and as part of your profile)
  • Password (stored securely as a hash ‚Äî we never see your actual password)

1.2 Profile Data

When you use the App, we automatically assign you:

  • An anonymous animal avatar (used for identity during polls)
  • Achievement badges earned through participation

1.3 Usage Data

We collect data related to your interactions with the App:

  • Pools created, joined, and voting history
  • Food suggestions and options contributed
  • App usage patterns (screen views, features used)

1.4 Device Information

We may collect:

  • Push notification tokens (for sending pool notifications)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Language preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide the service: Create and manage your account, enable pool creation and voting
  • Facilitate anonymous voting: Assign animal avatars for identity-hidden participation
  • Send notifications: Alert you about pool updates, voting deadlines, and results
  • Display results: Show vote breakdowns and winning choices
  • Improve the App: Understand usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
  • Award achievements: Track and display earned badges and medals

3. Anonymity & Voting Privacy

Anonymity is central to FoodPool's design:

  • During active polls, no one ‚Äî including pool creators ‚Äî can see who voted for what
  • Your real name is replaced by a random animal avatar in each pool
  • Vote data is associated with your anonymous identity, not your real profile
  • After a poll ends, participation is visible but individual vote choices remain anonymous

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be processed by:

  • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider (database, authentication, real-time sync)
  • Expo / React Native: Our app development framework (for push notifications)

These service providers are bound by their own privacy policies and process data only as necessary to provide their services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Database access is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Passwords are hashed and salted ‚Äî they are never stored in plain text
  • Authentication tokens are securely stored on your device
  • API endpoints are protected against unauthorized access

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Poll data: Voting history and pool records are retained indefinitely for your reference
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all personal data we hold about you (available in your profile)
  • Update: Modify your name, email, or profile information at any time
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from your profile settings
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications at any time through your device settings

8. Children's Privacy

FoodPool is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the App or via email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us
