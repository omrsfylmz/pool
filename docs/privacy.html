Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-02-01

FoodPool ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up, we may collect your email address and username to manage your account and pools.

Usage Data

We collect data regarding your interactions with the App, such as:

  • Voting history
  • Pools created
  • Restaurants/Dishes suggested

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality.
  • Synchronize votes in real-time.
  • Display results and voting breakdowns.

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information and ensure your voting data is handled securely.

4. Contact Us
