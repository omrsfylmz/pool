## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I join a pool?
Enter the unique code shared by the host on the home screen to join their room instantly.

### How is the winner decided?
The restaurant or dish with the most votes at the end of the voting session wins. In case of a tie, the app may prompt for a revote or choose randomly depending on settings.

### Can I change my vote?
Yes, you can change your vote as long as the voting phase is still active.

### How do I delete my account?
Please contact our support team at the email above to request account deletion.
