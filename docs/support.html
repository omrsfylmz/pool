Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a pool?
Enter the unique code shared by the host on the home screen to join their room instantly. No account is required for guests.
How is the winner decided?
The restaurant or dish with the most votes at the end of the voting session wins. In case of a tie, the app may prompt for a revote or choose randomly depending on your room settings.
Can I change my vote?
Yes, you can change your vote as long as the voting phase is still active. Once the timer runs out, votes are locked.
How do I delete my account?
Please contact our support team at omrsfylmz@gmail.com to request account deletion.
