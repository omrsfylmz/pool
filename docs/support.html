Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a lunch pool?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen, give your pool a title and optional description, select a voting duration (from 30 seconds to several hours), and tap "Start Voting". Your pool will be created instantly with a unique 6-digit join code.
How do I join a pool?
Tap "Join a Pool" on the home screen and enter the 6-digit code shared by the pool creator. You'll be added to the pool instantly and can start suggesting food options and voting.
How is the winner decided?
The food option with the most votes when the timer expires wins. In case of a tie, the app uses a random tiebreaker to determine the winner. The winning result and full vote breakdown are shown to all participants.
Can I change my vote?
Yes! You can change your vote as many times as you want while the voting timer is still active. Once the timer runs out, all votes are locked in and the winner is revealed.
Is my vote really anonymous?
Absolutely. Each participant is assigned a random animal avatar (like "Owl" or "Panda") for each pool. No one ‚Äî not even the pool creator ‚Äî can see who voted for what during or after the poll. Your real identity is completely hidden.
How do I add food suggestions?
Once you've joined a pool, tap "+ Add Your Idea" to suggest a food option. You can add a name, choose an icon, and write an optional note explaining why it's the best choice. All suggestions are anonymous.
How do I delete my account?
Go to your Profile, scroll down, and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. You can also contact us at omrsfylmz@gmail.com to request account deletion.
Do I need an account to join a pool?
Yes, you need a free FoodPool account to create or join pools. This ensures every participant has a unique anonymous identity and can access their voting history.
